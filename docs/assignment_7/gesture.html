Instructions

To activate the swipe pad, you'll need to

  1. Click (and hold) on the pad.
  2. Swipe in any direction
  3. Release mouse click

Please, not the system judges the swipe by comparing the start and stop positions for the down click and release. If you move your mouse in more than one direction (i.e. draw a shape like a circle), the system will not be able to provide an accurate swipe description. Also if your movement is too small, the system will display "No Movement".
